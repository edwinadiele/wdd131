Website Planning Document

Site Name

Mountain Adventure Hub

This site is designed for outdoor enthusiasts looking for hiking trails, camping spots, and adventure gear recommendations.

Site Purpose

The site provides information on the best hiking and camping locations, gear reviews, and trip-planning tips.

Scenarios

  • What are the top hiking trails in my area?
  • How do I prepare for a multi-day camping trip?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #005f73 (used for headings)

Secondary Color: #e63946 (used for buttons and accents)

Typography

Headings: Roboto

Body Text: Open Sans
